## 📐 Design Tokens Applied

- **Color System**: High-contrast Medical Blues: Primary (#00478d) & Secondary (#006a6a).
- **Typography**: Manrope (Headlines) & Inter (Body) dual-font architecture.
- **Surface Strategy**: Chronic-less sections defined by tonal shifts instead of rigid borders.
- **Visuals**: Premium AI-generated imagery tailored to the "The Clinical Sanctuary" theme.

## 💻 Tech Stack
- **Next.js 16 (App Router)**
- **Tailwind CSS 4.0**
- **TypeScript 5.0**
- **Lucide-React Icons**
- **Framer Motion for smoothness**

The local development server is active on [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000).
